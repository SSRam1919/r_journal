## â“ FAQ

### Q: Can I change item type after adding?
**A:** Currently, you need to delete and re-add. Future update will allow type changing.

### Q: Why is text black on some colors and white on others?
**A:** The app automatically calculates the best text color for readability based on background luminance.

### Q: Can I reorder items?
**A:** Not yet. Items appear in the order you add them. Future update will add drag-to-reorder.

### Q: How many items can I add?
**A:** No limit! Add as many as you need.

### Q: Can I toggle checkboxes in the card view?
**A:** Not yet. Checkboxes are read-only in cards. Tap the card to edit and toggle. This prevents accidental changes and improves performance.

### Q: What happens to old notes?
**A:** Old notes get a default white background. They still display correctly with the new text parsing.
